Introduction

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is a leading self-financing engineering college located at Kanjirappally, Kerala. Established in 2001 and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), the college offers quality technical education. It is known for experienced faculty, good infrastructure, and strong placement support.

Product – Academic & Student Offerings

  • B.Tech programs in CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical & Civil Engineering
  • M.Tech and postgraduate programs
  • Degree programs in BCA,INT MCA,REGULAR MCA
  • Experienced faculty, modern labs, library & hostels
  • Placements, mentoring, clubs and technical activities
